## dataset information:Bank Marketing
Source/Creators: The dataset was created by S. Moro, P. Rita, and P. Cortez. Their introductory paper is titled 'A data-driven approach to predict the success of bank telemarketing' published in Decision Support Systems in 2014.
Repository: UCI Machine Learning Repository.
UCI ID: 222
Name: Bank Marketing
Repository URL: [redacted link]
Abstract: The data is related to direct marketing campaigns (phone calls) of a Portuguese banking institution. The classification goal is to predict if the client will subscribe to a term deposit.
Area: Business
Tasks: Classification
Characteristics: Multivariate
Population/Num_Instances: The dataset contains 45,211 instances (clients).
Num_Features: 16 features.
Feature Types: Categorical, Integer.
Demographics: Includes 'Age', 'Occupation', 'Marital Status', 'Education Level'.
Target Column: 'y' (whether the client subscribed a term deposit).
Missing Values: The dataset has missing values, symbolized by 'NaN' (as observed in our X.info() output).
Year of Dataset Creation: 2014
Last Updated: Fri Aug 18 2023
Additional Info Summary: The marketing campaigns were based on phone calls. The goal is to predict if the client will subscribe (yes/no) to a term deposit. The dataset is the 'bank-full.csv' version mentioned in the additional info, containing all examples and 17 inputs (including the target 'y').
